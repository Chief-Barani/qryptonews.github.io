1

Intro 

Bitcoin Cash was created on August 1st 2017 
after hard fork of Bitcoin which resulted in the creation of 
a new blockchain and cryptocurrency.
It was originally proposed by Chinese Bitcoin mining pool.

2

Algorithm 

Bitcoin Cash transactions use a new signature hashing 
algorithm indicated by the flag SIGHASH_FORKID.
SigHash algorithm also provides replay protection,
hardware wallet security.

3

About 

It is peer-to-peer electronic cash fully decentralized, 
with no central bank and requires no trusted third parties.

4

Feature 

The main difference between BTC and BCH is
the block size, where Bitcoin allows for 1 MB(maximum),
Bitcoin Cash has a allowance of 32 MB per block.
