BCH hard forks & BTC reaches a new marketcap low

Bitcoin market cap falls below $100 billion for
			first time since October 2017. As of Thursday afternoon 
			in Asia, the market cap of Bitcoin was at $97,554,227,588.
			The Bitcoin Cash had begun its scheduled hard fork on Nov 15.
			The imminent BCH hard fork surely caused a stir in the community 
			with the prices reflecting the same. As a result, entire cryptocurrency 
			market capitalization got dropped by $15 billion within the last 24 hours.
