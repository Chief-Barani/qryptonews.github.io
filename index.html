Japanese Regulator Unveils Plan to Regulate Wallet 

Japan's top financial regulator, the Financial Services 
			Agency, has unveiled a plan to regulate cryptocurrency
			wallet services. The regulator has put forward a number of 
			regulatory measures as well as proposing how to implement them.
			The Financial Services Agency held its 9th cryptocurrency study 
			group meeting on Monday. The regulations for wallet services will be in 
			line with international standards for preventing money laundering & terrorism
