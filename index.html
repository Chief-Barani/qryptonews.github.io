Major US Museum Now accepts Crypto Payments

Great Lakes Science Center, a large museum &
			educational facility in downtown Cleveland, Ohio, 
			will begin accepting Bitcoin as a payment beginning 
			November 13. Museum of the Coastal Bend in Texas and 
			St. Petersburg Museum of History have been accepting BTC 
			for quite some time, and Great Lakes Science Center is the 
			third museum in the US to accept cryptocurrencies as a payment
